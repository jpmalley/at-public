During This Live Webinar, You Will Learn:

1
How to set boundaries.

Align your life with your needs, values, and expectations to truly live in integrity. Learn to protect and maintain your peace. Become a boundary badass and set & honor your boundaries with consistency!

2
How to assert yourself -- your thoughts, feelings, experience, and reality.

I'll teach you how to vulnerably & honestly share both your inner & outer world with assurance. No more feeling insecure when sharing and expressing your truth! No more hiding or dismissing your thoughts, feelings, experience, and reality!

3
How to declare your needs.

Directly declaring your needs to someone(s) often feels scary for us because it can feel that we come across as domineering or insensitive -- this is not true! I'll teach you how to not ask for, but declare what you need, and do it feeling empowered and secure. No more avoiding your physical and emotional needs!

4
How to ask for what you want.

Instead of hoping things will happen to work in your favor to provide you with what you want from someone(s), we need to take action and ask. Asking for what we want feels foreign and uncomfortable for many women and if we don't ask for what we want, we'll perpetually be unfulfilled and won't create the life we truly want for ourselves. I'll teach you how to no longer ignore what you want out of your one, precious life!

5
At the end of this webinar, you'll know exactly what to do to advocate for yourself with clarity.

No more uncertainty about what you need to do to advocate for yourself when initiating and navigating a hard conversation. STEP INTO THE SELF-ADVOCACY ARENA, MY FRIENDS!
